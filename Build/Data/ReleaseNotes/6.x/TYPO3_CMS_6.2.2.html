Release Notes for TYPO3 CMS 6.2.2

This document contains information about TYPO3 CMS 6.2.2 which was released on May 6th, 2014.

News

This release is an important bug fix release. It fixes several regressions introduced with 6.2.1. Users of 6.2.0 and 6.2.1 are strongly encouraged to update.

Upgrading

The usual upgrading procedure applies. No database updates are necessary.
